Prices

Tree Removal

Tree removal costs vary widely depending on factors like tree size, location, condition, and complexity. Generally, small trees (under 30 feet) might cost $150-$400 to remove, medium trees (30-60 feet) could range from $300-$1,200, while large trees (over 60 feet) might be $1,200-$3,000 or more. Additional fees may apply for stump removal or disposal.



Tree Maintenance

Tree maintenance pricing varies depending on factors like tree size, species, location, and services required. Basic services such as pruning and trimming may start at $100-$500 per tree. Larger trees or those needing more extensive care like disease treatment or emergency removal can cost upwards of $1,000 or more.



Tree Trimming

Tree trimming costs vary based on factors like tree size, location, and complexity. Small trees may cost $100-$300, while large or difficult-to-reach trees can range from $300-$1,500 or more. Additional factors like debris removal, permits, and emergency service may influence the final price. Getting multiple quotes helps ensure fair pricing.



Stump Removal

Stump removal costs range widely based on factors like stump size, location, accessibility, and method. Expect to pay between $60 to $350 per stump for manual removal and up to $500 or more for larger stumps or ones in challenging locations. Some companies charge per inch of diameter, averaging $2 to $5 per inch.



Stump Grinding

Stump grinding costs vary based on factors such as stump size, location, accessibility, and the number of stumps. On average, expect to pay between $100 to $400 per stump. Larger stumps or difficult access may incur higher costs. Some companies charge per inch of diameter, ranging from $2 to $4 per inch.
